ANNOUNCEMENT FOR PARENT'S MEETING


LYCEE SAINT ALEXANDRE SAULI DE MUHURA


12 July 2021
RE: PARENT -TEACHER CONFERENCE EVENINGS
TUESDAY 4 & THURSDAY 6 July, 10:00AM-12:30PM
Dear Parents/Guardians,
We would like to invite you to attend Individual Parent-Teacher Conferences. These
meetings are a valuable chance to meet with your child’s class teacher to discuss how
your child has settled in since the start of year, review their progress, and consider next steps in their learning.
In order to provide parents with the most convenient time possible with their child’s teachers, we use an appointment system throughout the school. This allows parents who may have several children at the school to make consecutive appointments rather than having to wait for long periods of time in between meetings. Each appointment is 10 minutes long and we would ask all parents to keep to these timings. All requests for appointments should be returned to your child’s class teacher by Friday 17 October. Confirmation of appointment times will be issued by Friday 24 October.
Where possible, we encourage children in Years 3-6 to take part in these meetings with their parents and class teacher. We ask however that you do not bring younger children to school or into these meetings as they often distract parents from the important discussions with teachers. Please make arrangements for your children to be collected from school and/or supervised by an adult while you are in your meetings.
If you are unable to attend on one of the designated evenings, please contact your child’s teacher who will arrange an alternative, mutually convenient appointment to see you.
